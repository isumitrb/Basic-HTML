ANIME Introduction

1. NARUTO

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is told in two parts – the first set in Naruto’s pre-teen years, and the second in his teens.

Serialized in Shueisha’s magazine, Weekly Shōnen Jump from 1999 to 2014, it has become one of the best-selling manga series with 250 million copies in circulation worldwide. The series has been adapted into an anime, which includes 220 episodes and a sequel series, Naruto: Shippuden.

2. DEATH NOTE

Death Note (デスノート, Desu Nōto) is a supernatural thriller fantasy manga written by Tsugumi Ōba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata. It follows Light Yagami, a high school student who finds a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it.

The series has been adapted into an anime, light novels, live-action films, and more. It explores themes of justice, morality, and the psychological conflict between Light and the detective L.

3. JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a sorcery school after swallowing a cursed object. He becomes the vessel for Sukuna, a powerful curse, and must find a way to defeat him while battling other curses.

The series is known for its balance of horror, action, and humor, with compelling characters and high stakes.

4. DEMON SLAYER

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons. The series has received critical acclaim for its art, storyline, and action scenes, and it includes a highly successful anime adaptation.

5. ONE PIECE

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they search for the ultimate treasure, the One Piece. It is one of the longest-running manga series and has been adapted into an anime that has become immensely popular worldwide.

6. BLEACH

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who becomes a Soul Reaper after meeting Rukia Kuchiki. The series explores his battles against evil spirits known as Hollows and the conflicts within the Soul Society.
